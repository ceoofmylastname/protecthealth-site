"Who's your HR department?" If the answer is "me, I guess," the business is in the majority — and carrying more risk than the owner usually realizes.

## What's the difference between HR functions and an HR department?

Every business with employees needs the functions: compliant hiring paperwork, a handbook, documented onboarding, wage-and-hour compliance, and defensible termination practices. Almost no small business needs a salaried department to deliver them. The functions are a service problem, not a staffing problem, until headcount reaches the 50-100 range.
